Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose

It is blood alone that can pay the price of freedom.

Here we take a glimpse of Netaji's timeline:

  • Jan 23, 1897 - Subhas Chandra Bose is born
  • Mar 29, 1911 - Bose admitted to Presidency college
  • Mar 28, 1918 - Recieves is B.A. in philosophy
  • Apr 24, 1919 - Leaves India for Great Britain
  • May 29, 1919 - Bose takes the ICS exam
  • May 20, 1923 - Elected leader of the All India Youth Council and secretary of the Bengal State Congress
  • Sep 14, 1925 - Bose is arrested
  • Mar 30, 1927 - Bose is released from prison and began working with Nehru
  • May 28, 1930 - Bose becomes mayor of Calcutta
  • Feb 28, 1938 - Bose accepts nomination as the INC presidential candidate
  • Oct 8, 1939 - Bose organizes the All India Forward Bloc
  • Jan 19, 1941 - Bose escapes from India after it becomesto hostile towards him
  • Aug 18, 1945 - Subhas Chandra Bose dissapears

"Inner religious explorations continued to be a part of his adult life. This set him apart from the slowly growing number of atheistic socialists and communists who dotted the Indian landscape."

-- Historian of South Asia Leonard Gordon
